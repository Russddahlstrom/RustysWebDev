Resume Russell D. Dahlstrom

Born and raised in Pocatello, Idaho, I grew up living a farm life. My mother, daughter of a farmer, and my father raised me right, learning the values of hard work and honesty from a young age. I know not to do a half-crap job because of the envioronment in which I was raised.
